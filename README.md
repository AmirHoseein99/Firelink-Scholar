# Firelink Scholar

**Ah... another soul drawn to the dying flame.**

**Hark, and lend thine ear.**

Many ages have passed beneath the fading flame.
Kingdoms have risen, and kingdoms have fallen.

The songs of gods have faded.
The names of heroes have been forgotten.
The tales of the damned have turned to ash.

Yet I remain.

I am the Firelink Scholar, keeper of forgotten chronicles and guardian of memories left behind.

Through ancient scriptures and remnants of ages past, I seek the truths hidden within this cursed cycle.

Come, sit beside the flame.

Ask, and the ashes shall speak.
